The Patent!

Method of obtention of faceted fluorapatite nanocrystals

The invention discloses the obtention of fluorapatite (FA) nanocrystals from enameloid sections of shark teeth for application as bone filler material in orthopedical surgery, enamel regeneration and other dental and maxillofacial treatments in the medical field.
The present invention is an isolated faceted fluorapatite nanocrystal. In a preferred embodiment, the fluorapatite nanocrystal includes traces of Mg, Na, K y Cl, and in another preferred embodiment shows a size of 35 to 700nm.

Patent issuer and number: eu EP 2853622 B1 20160113
Patent date: Nov 20, 2013.

In college!

Mining engineering is an engineering discipline (not a very popular area) that applies science and technology to the extraction of minerals from the earth. Mining engineering is associated with many other areas, such as mathematics, geology, geotechnical engineering, mineral processing and metallurgy.

Mining engineering degree allows certifying professional competencies of the engineer graduating:

It integrates different disciplines for problem solving and development of increasingly complex projects.

It efficiently and innovatively solves problems of the mining industry under the concept of continuous improvement.

It uses calculation, simulation, design, management, analysis and audit methodologies in the production, transformation, storage, transportation and use of energy and non-energy raw materials.
